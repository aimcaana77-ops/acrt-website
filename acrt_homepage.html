Our Mission

To deliver standardized, reliable, and life-saving emergency response training that increases survival from cardiac arrest and other emergencies in our community.

Who we are

ACRT is a training center run under AIMCAANA, providing BLS and first response courses with certified instructors.

Why it matters

Early CPR and defibrillation dramatically improve survival. We teach practical skills people can use today.
